Tomato Soup

Description

A rich, smooth, comforting soup that's quick to make when you have ripe summer tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Chicken broth
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil the tomatoes, onion, garlic, and broth.
  2. Run the mixture through a food mill into a large bowl.
  3. Make a roux.
  4. Add the tomato mixture and season.
